Meet William

William Alvarez is a jack of all trades designer based in the Coal Harbour area of Downtown Vancouver. He is currently a first year student at BCIT, enrolled in the Digital Design and Development program. He has dabbled in Digital Media software such as Photoshop and 3DS Max throughout high school and wishes to transition into a career in Interaction Design. He is intrigued by the front-end development of applications and wishes to explore this further during his stay in BCIT.
